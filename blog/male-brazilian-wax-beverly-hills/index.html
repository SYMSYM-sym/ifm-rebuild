What the service includes

The male Brazilian wax covers five zones: the pubic mound, the shaft, the scrotum, the perineum, and the area between the buttocks. Rene' applies hard wax in small sections and removes it by hand without strips. The wax adheres to the hair shaft and pulls coarse growth cleanly from the root. Each section takes 60 to 90 seconds to set before removal.

You undress from the waist down and lie on a treatment table covered with fresh linens. Rene' works through the zones methodically, starting with the pubic mound and moving to the shaft and scrotum. The final section addresses the area between the buttocks, which requires you to shift position. The entire appointment runs 30 to 45 minutes depending on density and whether this is your first wax or a maintenance visit.

Most men feel discomfort during the first pull in each zone. The sensation fades as the appointment progresses because the skin adjusts to the rhythm. Coarse hair on the scrotum and perineum takes more precision than the pubic mound, but the process is the same: apply wax, wait for it to set, remove in one motion.

First appointment versus maintenance visits

Your first Brazilian wax takes longer than follow-up appointments because the hair is denser and the follicles are stronger. Rene' may go over certain sections twice to catch shorter strands that did not lift on the first pass. The scrotum and shaft require extra attention because the skin is thinner and the hair grows in multiple directions.

Maintenance appointments move faster. Hair grows back finer and sparser after repeated waxing, which means fewer passes and less time on the table. Most men book every three to four weeks to stay ahead of regrowth. If you wait longer than six weeks, the hair reaches the same density as a first appointment and the process resets.

What to expect after the appointment

Redness and sensitivity last two to four hours after a Brazilian wax. Avoid tight clothing, heavy exercise, and hot showers for 24 hours. The skin needs time to close the follicles and calm inflammation. Most men feel normal by the next morning.

Ingrown hairs occur when new growth curls under the skin instead of breaking through the surface. Exfoliate gently with a washcloth or body scrub three days after your appointment and continue twice a week. Do not pick at ingrown hairs or use sharp tools to extract them. If an ingrown becomes inflamed, apply a warm compress and let it resolve on its own.

Results last two to four weeks depending on your hair growth cycle. Some men see regrowth in 14 days, others in 28. The timeline depends on genetics, age, and how often you wax. Consistent appointments train the follicles to produce finer hair, which extends the smooth period over time.

FAQ

Frequently asked

Does the Brazilian wax hurt more than other services?

The scrotum and perineum are more sensitive than the chest or back, but the discomfort is brief. Each section takes 60 to 90 seconds to remove. Most men describe the sensation as sharp but tolerable.

Can I book the same day if I am visiting Beverly Hills?

Same-day appointments are rare because the schedule fills quickly. Book at least 48 hours in advance, especially for weekend slots. Weekday mornings have the most availability.

How long does hair need to be before a Brazilian wax?

Hair should be at least a quarter inch long, roughly the length of a grain of rice. If you shaved recently, wait two to three weeks before booking. Rene' will assess length during the appointment.

Is the West Hollywood location easy to reach from Beverly Hills?

Yes. The drive takes seven minutes via Robertson Boulevard or Santa Monica Boulevard. The salon is near Cedars-Sinai and the Beverly Center, just north of the Sunset Strip.
