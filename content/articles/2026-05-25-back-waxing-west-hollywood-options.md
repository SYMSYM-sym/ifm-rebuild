Back waxing in West Hollywood includes three main options: half back, full back, and shoulders only. A half back covers the lower or upper section depending on where you carry the most growth. A full back runs from the neck to the waistline and includes the shoulder blades. Shoulder-only waxing targets the cap and upper trapezius. Most men book a full back when they want consistent results and choose half back when growth is concentrated in one zone.

## What each option covers

A full back wax removes hair from the base of the neck to the waistline. The practitioner applies hard wax in sections and works from the shoulders down. The service takes 35–45 minutes and includes the shoulder blades, mid-back, and lower lumbar region. Men who carry hair across the entire back book this option to avoid visible lines or uneven texture.

A half back covers either the upper back (neck to mid-spine) or the lower back (mid-spine to waistline). Upper half appointments focus on the shoulder blades and trapezius. Lower half appointments target the lumbar region and sides. The session runs 20–30 minutes. Men who grow hair primarily in one area choose this option to reduce appointment time and cost.

Shoulder-only waxing removes growth from the shoulder cap and upper trapezius. The practitioner applies wax to the deltoid area and pulls in the direction of growth. The appointment takes 15–20 minutes. Men who want clean shoulders for tank tops or who carry minimal back hair book this service.

## How to decide which option to book

Look at where your hair grows thickest. If you see consistent coverage from your neck to your waistline, book a [full back wax](/#services). If growth concentrates above or below the mid-spine, a half back delivers the same smooth result in less time. If hair appears only on the shoulder caps, book shoulders only.

Consider how you dress. Men who wear open-collar shirts or go shirtless at the beach often prefer full back waxing to avoid visible lines. Men who wear fitted T-shirts may only need the upper half. Men who layer clothing year-round sometimes choose lower half waxing because the upper back stays covered.

Think about maintenance. A full back wax lasts three to four weeks before regrowth becomes noticeable. A half back or shoulder wax follows the same timeline but may show contrast if adjacent areas remain hairy. Booking the same option each visit keeps results consistent.

## What happens during the appointment

You arrive at the salon on Larrabee Street in West Hollywood and check in at the private suite. The practitioner asks you to remove your shirt and lie face-down on the treatment table. They assess your hair type and skin sensitivity before applying any wax.

The practitioner applies hard wax in small sections. Hard wax adheres to the hair shaft rather than the skin, which reduces irritation on broad areas like the back. They wait 20–30 seconds for the wax to set, then pull it off in one motion. The process repeats until the selected area is clear.

After the wax, the practitioner applies a cooling gel to reduce redness. They check for stray hairs and remove them with tweezers if needed. You dress and receive aftercare instructions. Most men see mild redness that fades within six hours.

Appointments for a full back run 35–45 minutes. Half back takes 20–30 minutes. Shoulders only take 15–20 minutes. The practitioner works at a steady pace and does not rush. You can ask questions or request a break at any point.

## Frequently asked

**Does back waxing hurt more than other areas?**
The back has fewer nerve endings than the chest or groin, so most men report less discomfort. Pain increases near the spine and shoulder blades where skin is thinner. The practitioner applies tension and pulls quickly to reduce the sensation.

**How long does back waxing last?**
Results last three to four weeks before regrowth becomes visible. Hair grows back finer and softer than it was before waxing. Booking every four weeks maintains smooth skin year-round.

**Can I book a full back and chest in one appointment?**
Yes. The practitioner schedules 60–75 minutes for combined back and chest waxing. Most men book both areas together to keep the torso consistent. You lie face-down for the back and face-up for the chest.

**What should I wear to a back waxing appointment?**
Wear a loose T-shirt or button-down shirt. Avoid tight clothing that will rub against freshly waxed skin. Bring a clean shirt if you plan to go somewhere after the appointment. The practitioner provides disposable draping if needed.
